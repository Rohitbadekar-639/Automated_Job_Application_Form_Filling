Privacy Policy for Job Application Autofiller

Last updated: 8 July 2024

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Job Application Autofiller Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

The Job Application Autofiller extension collects and stores the following information that you provide:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Educational information
  • Work experience
  • Skills
  • Professional profile links (LinkedIn, portfolio, GitHub)

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the purpose of automatically filling out job application forms. We do not use your information for any other purpose, nor do we share or sell your data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All information you provide is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Your data is not transmitted to or stored on any external servers. We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or alteration.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information
  • Delete your personal information from the extension

You can exercise these rights at any time through the extension's user interface.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
